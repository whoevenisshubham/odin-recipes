Traditional Algerian noodles with chicken, vegetables, and warm spices.

Rechta is a beloved Algerian noodle dish often enjoyed during family gatherings and religious holidays. It features handmade or fresh noodles served with a savory broth of chicken, chickpeas, and spiced vegetables. The sauce is gently infused with North African spices like turmeric, cinnamon, and ras el hanout, offering a comforting, flavorful experience rooted in Maghrebi tradition.
